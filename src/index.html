To:You From:Me

Intro

5 years ago I moved to Berlin to study advertising and media at Miami AD School and to resolve a query about my professional future. The last thing I wanted was a static profession. Leaving my hometown in San Francisco to start a new life was a stretch, but I couldn't just let my potential be set aside and sit in the margin. I had to be bold and brace myself for the biggest life and professional transition I could make.

I was fixed on building up a strong skill set that does not block my creative nature, yet I needed a way to optimize my path forward.


Finding myself in my first position as a creative, after studying font-families, animations, and scripts, and making a career in advertising the object of my focus, 2020 sadly turned out to be Covid-19. I was no longer sitting at the advertising table in Berlin.

So very far from the reaching the height of my professional potential, I had to admit I was in a sticky situation. Thinking about how far I had come, and the idea that I would not be able to flex my talents, I hit a breakpoint. The moment challenged me to be active and find a way to justify all the risks and sacrifices I took to make this journey and go against some of my parents' input.


Navigation in the Corona era was one of the main things I was missing. My goal was to fill in the gap that was left from my broken quest, and to align with a new flow that would unset the inherit ebb.

Then, I noticed the title for the DCI and I knew I was heading for the absolute answer. Alas, there was a way to get myself back inline. Finally, some form to put space between my past trouble and future successes!


Thanks to the DCI class I see a clear way to link my skill sets together that would size me up for becoming a developer with a unique background, style, and mental map. I look forward to being part of the developer community in the next span of my life and learn how I continue to transform.
