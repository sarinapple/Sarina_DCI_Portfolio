How I got here

My name is Sarina, and 5 years ago I moved to Berlin to study advertising and media at Miami AD School to resolve this query about my future.


The last thing I wanted was a static profession.


Leaving my hometown in San Francisco to start a new life was a stretch, but I could not let my potential be set aside and sit in the margin. I had to be bold and brace myself for the scale of this life transition.

I was fixed on building up a strong skill set that does not block my creative nature, yet I needed a way to optimize my path forward.


Finding myself in my first position as an intern, after studying font-families, animations, and scripts, and making a career in advertising the object of my focus, 2020 sadly turned out to be Covid-19. My seat at the creative table vanished.

Farther from the height of my potential than I ever imagined, admittedly, I was in a sticky situation.


To imagine that I would not be able to flex my talents led me to a breakpoint. The moment challenged me to be active and justify all the risks I assumed, and even going against some of my parents' input.


Navigation was the main thing I was missing. My goal was to fill in the gap that was left from my broken quest, and to align with a new flow that would unset the inherit ebb.

One day I noticed the title for the Digital Career Institute and I knew I was heading for the absolute answer. Alas, there was a way to get myself back inline. Finally, some form to put space between my past trouble and future successes!


Thanks to the DCI class I see a clear way to link my skill sets together that would size me up for becoming a developer with a unique background, style, and mental map. I look forward to being part of the developer community in the next span of my life and learn how I continue to transform.
